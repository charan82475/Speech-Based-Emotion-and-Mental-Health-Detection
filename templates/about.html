About Emotion Novelty Detection

This AI system analyzes human voice in real-time to determine whether the expressed emotion is Normal (typical, expected patterns) or Abnormal (novel, unusual, or outlier emotional expression). Useful for mental health monitoring, anomaly detection in communication, and affective computing research.

Real-Time Voice Analysis

Captures live speech and processes it instantly to detect emotional normality.

Custom Deep Learning Model

Powered by a fine-tuned PyTorch model trained to distinguish normal vs abnormal emotional patterns.

Local & Private Processing

All audio stays on your device — no data is sent to external servers.

Confidence Feedback

Shows prediction confidence so you can evaluate reliability of each result.
